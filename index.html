About this place

Who I am and what I do

Hello and welcome! This is the second home of Emil. I make things and do stuff. Most of it with data. Some of it is cool. hvor.io is where I (try) to put some of my bigger projects / ideas / things. (It also gives me a second place to try stuff out)


I have a background in geography and I have worked with data for a while now. Most of what you'll find is exploring ways of analysing and visualising data. I do this for fun and to learn new things - and I hope it will help you learn new things as well. My main focuses are programming, statistical inference, machine learning and some deep learning.


Don't hesitate to visit my personal profile, you'll probably find some cool stuff there as well. I hope you find my sites fun.
